About me

As a web developer and graphic designer, I am driven by a passion for IT and new technologies. Graduated from the African Institute of Informatics IAI-TOGO with a degree in Software Engineering and Information Systems, I was able to dive into the world of programming and web development. I had the opportunity to work on various applications, solving a multitude of problems and providing user-friendly solutions

My interest in aesthetics led me to explore the field of graphic design. Today, I consider this skill as a major asset, allowing me to combine web development and design to offer solutions that are not only effective and secure, but also visually attractive for users.

However, my interest in IT is not limited to web development and graphics. I am also involved in project management, where my versatility, my leadership and my sense of teamwork are put to good use.

Although I am still at the beginning of my journey in the IT field, my goal is clear: to provide quality service, appreciated throughout the world. I am determined to continue learning, adapting and innovating to achieve this goal
